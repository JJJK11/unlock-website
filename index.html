Maturity Model

Where is your
organization today?

We assess your current operating maturity and define the most direct path to higher capability, governance, and performance.

Level 01

Traditional

  • 70%+ manual workflows
  • Automation below 10%
  • Siloed systems, no APIs
  • No formal governance
Level 02

Transitional

  • 1–3 pilots with baseline KPIs
  • 10–30% targeted automation
  • Initial governance and RASCI
  • 80–90% data accuracy
Level 03

Augmented

  • Standardized processes across 3+ functions
  • 30–60% workflow automation
  • Governed decision forums and audit trails
  • Operational KPIs tied to platform data
Level 04

Intelligent Enterprise

  • Integrated operating model with closed-loop performance
  • 60%+ automation with bounded autonomy
  • Real-time observability across operations
  • Continuous improvement pipeline active
Our Approach

Structured transformation
framework

Transformation fails when it's treated as a single event. We run a five-phase operating discipline that moves from diagnosis through institutionalized governance — each phase producing measurable capability, governed by data, and directly tied to enterprise outcomes.

01

Discovery & Context

Systems, workflows, operating model, readiness

We build a factual, data-driven view of your current state — systems, workflows, operating model, governance, and organizational readiness. This includes stakeholder interviews, process mapping, technology inventory, and a control-point analysis across business and IT. The output is a documented baseline with clear boundaries, dependencies, and readiness scores that inform every decision downstream.

Stakeholder interviewsProcess mappingTechnology inventoryControl-point analysisReadiness scoring
02

Design & Governance

Operating model, RASCI, risk, decision forums

Discovery findings are translated into an actionable transformation architecture — target operating model, accountability structures (RASCI), risk posture, decision forums, and change management framework. We define governance cadence, escalation paths, and success metrics before any build work begins, ensuring the program has structural integrity from day one.

Target operating modelRASCIRisk postureDecision forumsChange architecture
03

Build & Integrate

Platforms, automation, AI, security, observability

We configure platforms, automation layers, AI capabilities, and integration pipelines aligned to your enterprise systems. This phase covers solution architecture, development sprints, API orchestration, security hardening, observability instrumentation, and UAT. Every component is built against the governance framework established in Phase 02 — no shadow IT, no ungoverned deployments.

Solution architectureAPI orchestrationSecurity hardeningObservabilityUAT
04

Scale & Adopt

Rollout, training, playbooks, adoption tracking

Pilots transition into production through structured rollout — phased deployment, role-based training, operational playbooks, and change management embedded in the business. We run adoption tracking, feedback loops, and performance benchmarks to ensure the organization absorbs the change rather than reverting to prior patterns. This is where transformation becomes operational reality.

Phased deploymentRole-based trainingPlaybooksAdoption trackingPerformance benchmarks
05

Govern & Evolve

Metrics, review cycles, benefits realization, continuous improvement

We institutionalize governance, performance metrics, review cycles, and benefits realization tracking. This includes establishing continuous improvement pipelines, quarterly maturity assessments, and executive reporting that ties transformation outcomes directly to enterprise KPIs. The goal is a self-sustaining operating discipline — not a dependency on external consultants.

GovernanceKPI trackingMaturity assessmentsExecutive reportingContinuous improvement
